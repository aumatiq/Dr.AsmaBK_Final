Scroll to the Book Appointment section below. Select a date, pick an available time slot, fill in your details, and confirm. You'll receive a Patient ID instantly — save it to access your records.

Your Patient ID (format: PT-2026-0001) is assigned automatically when you book your first appointment. Use it with your registered phone number to log into the Patient Portal and view your appointments, test results, and health records.

Click My Records in the navigation or scroll to the Patient Portal section. Log in with your Patient ID and phone number. Doctor-approved results appear there for download. You can also upload your own scan files for doctor review.
